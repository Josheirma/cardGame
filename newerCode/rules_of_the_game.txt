Blackjack 5 Card Dealer:
Rules of the game:
Cards are dealt with players first and then the dealer.
Try to score more than the dealer or score twenty-one to win with a blackjack.  
Scores over twenty-one bust and the player or dealer loses the bet wagered.
Blackjack scores double the amount.
Two tens can be divided to play with two hands instead of one.
Tens, Jacks, Queens, and Kings are all represented by card ten.
If a player has five cards the player wins automatically.
A Player's aces can be counted as one or eleven.
The dealer's aces always count as one and the dealer is dealt a maximum of five cards.
